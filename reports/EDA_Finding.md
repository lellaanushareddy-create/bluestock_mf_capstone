# EDA Findings Summary

## Key Findings

1. NAV values showed a steady growth trend over time.
2. AUM increased significantly across major fund houses.
3. SIP inflows recorded consistent monthly growth.
4. Equity funds attracted the highest investor participation.
5. Category-wise inflows varied across fund categories.
6. Folio counts increased steadily, indicating industry growth.
7. Financial and Technology sectors dominated portfolio allocations.
8. Investor participation was strongest in Tier-1 cities.
9. Most investors completed KYC verification.
10. Digital payment modes were widely used.

## Conclusion

The mutual fund industry demonstrated strong growth in investor participation, SIP adoption, AUM expansion, and portfolio diversification.
